About Me

I'm Theo, a 35-year-old architect based in Manhattan, New York

As an architect with a deep passion for creating spaces that inspire and uplift, I’ve spent over a decade turning visions into reality. My journey began with a fascination for blending form and function, and since then, I’ve had the privilege of designing homes, offices, and community spaces that connect people to their environments. I believe architecture isn’t just about buildings—it’s about shaping experiences and enhancing lives. My portfolio reflects a dedication to thoughtful design, sustainable practices, and a love for unique details that make each project stand out. I’m always excited to take on new challenges, working closely with clients to bring their dreams to life in creative and practical ways.
